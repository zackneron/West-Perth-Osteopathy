What is Osteopathy?

Osteopaths consider the effects of gravity on posture, musculoskeletal injuries, fluid dynamics of the body and function of the nervous system. We also recognise how body structure can influence the function of organ systems involved in breathing, digestion, energy production waste removal, immunity and reproduction. All of these elements are important for maintaining good health in body, mind and spirit.

What is Naturopathy?

Naturopathy is a preventative practice which assesses and assists with conditions of both body and mind. Effective in the treatment of both acute and chronic health conditions, naturopathic treatment may include nutritional medicine, dietary & lifestyle advice, herbal medicine, and vitamin/mineral supplementation. Because of its holistic nature, individuals of all ages, with all varieties of health issues and conditions, may benefit from naturopathic care.

Physical Freedom for Over 60s

Osteopathic treatments aim to improve the quality and range of motion in joints throughout the body, improve blood circulation and lymphatic drainage, and function of the nervous system. It is particularly suited to the treatment of aged people as these techniques are gentle and non-invasive.
