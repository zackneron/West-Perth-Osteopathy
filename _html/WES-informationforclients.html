What is Osteopathy?

Osteopathy - An integrated hands on approach for the treatment of Neurological and musculo-skeletal conditions for all ages. Offering effective pain relief and freedom of movement for all parts of the body. With combined experience, knowledge and compassion, offering the right treatment and advice all manner of acute or chronic conditions such as: ankle sprains, carpal tunnel syndrome, lower back discogenic pain, wry neck, headaches and migraines.

What is Naturopathy?

Naturopathy is a preventative practice which assesses and assists with conditions of both body and mind. Naturopathic medicine sees conventional health sciences integrated with natural therapies and traditional medicines to balance a patient’s mental, emotional and physical states. Injury, fatigue, stress, insomnia, allergies, chronic illness and dependence on pharmaceuticals are all signs that your body’s physiology needs restoration.

What do Osteos treat?

Working with a holistic approach to diagnosis, treatment and management, our osteopaths can treat a wide range of conditions using a variety of techniques. We believe that only by treating the individual, not just the symptoms, can problems be alleviated and a true sense of wellbeing restored. Because of its holistic nature, individuals of all ages, with all varieties of health issues and conditions, may benefit from osteopathic treatment.

Osteopathy for Children

Osteopathic care is safe, gentle and effective without the use of drugs or surgical treatment. It can aid the young body to adapt to growth-related changes which can prevent various other health issues. It can help your child become a healthy child and, eventually, a healthy adult. At West Perth Osteopathy our Osteopaths treat babies, children and teenagers for a range of conditions. Efficient, early therapy can prevent various other troubles from occurring.
